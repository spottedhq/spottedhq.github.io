**Teachable** is for creators who think of themselves as course entrepreneurs. Built-in sales funnels, one-click upsells, abandoned checkout recovery, and native affiliate management. It's a sales machine that happens to host courses.

**Thinkific** is for educators who want a flexible course builder with zero transaction fees on every plan. More content types, a reusable video library, and built-in communities. It's a teaching platform that happens to sell courses.

| **Free tier** | No (7-day trial) | No (14-day trial) |

## Pick Teachable if...

You care more about conversion rates than course design. You want built-in upsells, abandoned cart recovery, and affiliate management without third-party tools. You're selling 1-3 courses and want a polished, simple interface. You can afford the Builder plan ($69/mo) to avoid that 7.5% fee.

## Pick Thinkific if...

You're building a large course catalog with diverse content types. You want zero transaction fees from day one. You need built-in communities, assignments, and surveys without external tools. You want unlimited courses at a lower entry price ($36/mo vs $69/mo).

## The details

**Pricing trap:** Teachable looks cheaper ($29 vs $36), but the Starter plan takes 7.5% of every sale. Sell $2,000/mo in courses and that's $150 in fees on top of your $29 subscription. Thinkific's $36 Basic plan with 0% fees is cheaper for anyone making real revenue. Teachable's 0% starts at Builder ($69/mo).

**Course building:** Thinkific is more flexible. Native surveys, assignments, multimedia lessons, and a video library that lets you reuse uploads across courses. Teachable's editor is clean but more limited. You'll need Google Forms for surveys and won't get a reusable media library.

**Marketing tools:** Teachable wins. One-click upsells, order bumps, abandoned checkout emails, and a native affiliate program are built in. Thinkific has an app store with 90+ integrations, but the sales tooling isn't as tight out of the box.

**Community:** Thinkific offers built-in communities where students interact, plus live events. Teachable added community features but they're less mature.

**Coaching:** Teachable has dedicated coaching products with milestone tracking. Thinkific handles coaching through live events and communities but doesn't have a specific coaching product type.

## FAQ

**Do either take a cut of my sales?** Thinkific never does. Teachable takes 7.5% on Starter but 0% from Builder ($69/mo) up. Standard credit card processing fees apply on both.

**Can I migrate between them?** Yes, but manually. No one-click migration tool exists. You'll rebuild courses and re-import students. Plan it during a quiet period.

**Which is better for coaching?** Teachable. It has a dedicated coaching product with milestone tracking. Thinkific supports coaching through communities and live events but it's not a first-class feature.
