## The details

**Pricing:** Both cost $20/mo. Claude Pro edges ahead with a $17/mo annual billing option. ChatGPT offers a cheaper entry at $8/mo (Go plan) if you don't need full Plus features. Both have premium tiers at $100-200/mo for heavy users.

**Writing quality:** Claude produces more natural prose and is less likely to fall into formulaic patterns. For editing, summarization, and working with long documents, Claude has a measurable edge. ChatGPT is strong at structured reasoning and math.

**Tool ecosystem:** ChatGPT wins on breadth. DALL-E 3, web browsing, file analysis, custom GPTs, plugin marketplace, task automation. Claude is more focused: strong web research, Google Workspace integration, MCP connectors, and the project system for persistent context.

**Context window:** This is Claude's strongest advantage. 1M tokens means you can upload a 300-page PDF and ask about page 247 without the model losing track. ChatGPT's 128K is large by industry standards but can't match this for truly large documents. If you work with long contracts, codebases, or manuscripts, this difference is the deciding factor.
