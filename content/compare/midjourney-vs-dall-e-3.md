## The details

**Pricing:** If you only need image generation, Midjourney Basic at $10/mo is cheapest. But ChatGPT Plus at $20/mo bundles DALL-E 3 with GPT-5, web browsing, and code tools. The Standard plan ($30/mo) with unlimited Relax mode is the sweet spot for heavy Midjourney users.

**Image quality:** Midjourney wins on composition, lighting, and mood. DALL-E 3 wins on prompt accuracy and text rendering. Ask for "a red bicycle against a blue wall with a cat in the basket and a sign that says OPEN" and DALL-E 3 nails every element. Midjourney makes it prettier but might drop details.

**Ease of use:** DALL-E 3 has zero learning curve. Type what you want in ChatGPT. Midjourney requires learning parameters (aspect ratios, stylize values, chaos settings) to get the best results. The Discord community helps, but there's real investment needed.

**Commercial use:** Both allow commercial use on paid plans. Midjourney requires a paid subscription. DALL-E 3 through ChatGPT Plus is also cleared for commercial work.

## FAQ

**Is Midjourney free?** No. Cheapest plan is $10/mo ($8/mo annual). No free tier.

**Can DALL-E 3 match Midjourney's quality?** For artistic, cinematic images, no. For accuracy and text in images, it's better. Depends what you're optimizing for.

**What about Stable Diffusion?** It's free and open-source, but you need a decent GPU and technical comfort. For most people, Midjourney or DALL-E 3 is the easier path.
