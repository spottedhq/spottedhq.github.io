## The details

**Pricing:** Copy.ai is cheaper for most people. A 3-person team pays $69/mo on Jasper Pro vs $29/mo on Copy.ai. That gap adds up.

**Writing quality:** Jasper produces more polished long-form content, especially once you train its brand voice. Copy.ai is better for quick short-form stuff where you need options fast.

**Ease of use:** Copy.ai wins. Pick a template, fill in the brief, get results. Jasper takes longer to set up but the payoff is better output.

**Automation:** Copy.ai's Agents plan ($249/mo) lets you build multi-step content workflows. Jasper doesn't have anything like it. Most people won't need this, but if you do, it's a differentiator.

**Is Jasper worth $69/mo?** If brand consistency directly affects your revenue, yes. If you're just writing occasional blog posts, probably not.
