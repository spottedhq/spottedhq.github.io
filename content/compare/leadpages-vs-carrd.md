## What Is Leadpages?

Leadpages is a landing page and lead generation platform built specifically for marketers and small businesses that need pages designed to convert visitors into customers. Founded in 2012, it has carved out a solid position as the go-to tool for entrepreneurs, coaches, consultants, and small marketing teams who need landing pages without hiring a developer.

The core product is a drag-and-drop page builder with over 200 professionally designed templates, all optimized for conversion based on data from millions of pages. What separates Leadpages from generic website builders is its marketing DNA: built-in **A/B split testing** lets you test headlines, layouts, and CTAs to find what converts best. **Leadmeter**, their AI-powered tool, analyzes your page in real time and suggests improvements before you publish. Analytics dashboards show conversion rates, traffic sources, and lead counts without needing Google Analytics.

Pricing runs across three tiers. **Standard** ($49/month, or $37/month billed annually) covers the basics: unlimited landing pages, pop-ups, alert bars, free custom domain hosting, and email support. **Pro** ($99/month, or $74/month annually) adds A/B testing, online payments, email trigger links, and priority support -- this is the plan most serious marketers choose. **Conversion** ($697/month) is the white-glove tier with a dedicated conversion expert, full funnel audit, and custom-built campaigns. All plans include a 14-day free trial with full features.

Leadpages also includes an AI engine that generates headlines, copy suggestions, and content variations. Integrations cover all the major email platforms (Mailchimp, Kit, ActiveCampaign, HubSpot), payment processors (Stripe), webinar tools, and CRMs via Zapier.

## What Is Carrd?

Carrd is a one-page website builder created by a solo developer (AJ) that has quietly become one of the most popular tools on the internet for simple, beautiful single-page sites. It powers millions of sites -- from freelancer portfolios and link-in-bio pages to product launches and event signups. Its appeal is radical simplicity at a price that borders on absurd.

The **Free plan** lets you build and publish up to 3 sites on `.carrd.co` subdomains with core features and Carrd branding. That is genuinely enough for many people. The paid tiers are all billed annually with no monthly option:

- **Pro Lite** ($9/year) removes Carrd branding, gives you unbranded `.crd.co` URLs, and keeps the 3-site limit.
- **Pro Standard** ($19/year) is the most popular plan. You get up to 10 sites, custom domain support with free SSL, and forms -- including signup forms that integrate with Mailchimp, Kit, Brevo, HubSpot, Klaviyo, beehiiv, and a dozen other email platforms. Payment forms (via Stripe or PayPal) are also included.
- **Pro Plus** ($49/year) raises the limit to 25 sites and adds developer-friendly features: custom forms, advanced settings, and the ability to download unminified source code of your sites.

Carrd offers a 7-day free trial of Pro Plus features -- no credit card required -- which is one of the most frictionless trial experiences in the website builder space.

The design philosophy is intentionally constrained. Carrd builds one-page sites only. There is no blog engine, no multi-page navigation, no e-commerce cart. This constraint is also its strength: every template is designed to look stunning as a single scrolling page, and the editor is so simple that most people have a site live within 15-20 minutes.

### Features

**Leadpages wins on:** A/B split testing, conversion rate analytics, Leadmeter AI optimization, multi-step forms, payment processing at scale, pop-ups and alert bars, integration depth (native connections to 40+ marketing tools), Facebook ad builder, and the overall sophistication of the page builder for marketing use cases.

**Carrd wins on:** simplicity (live site in under 20 minutes), price (nothing comes close), design quality for minimal one-page sites, speed and performance (ultra-lightweight pages load instantly), no vendor lock-in (Pro Plus lets you download your site's source code), and the sheer value proposition of $19/year for 10 custom-domain sites with forms and payments.

### Ease of Use

Carrd is one of the easiest website builders ever made. The editor is visual, intuitive, and intentionally limited -- you cannot get lost because there are fewer things to configure. Pick a template, swap the text and images, connect your domain, publish. Most people are done in 15-20 minutes.

Leadpages is also drag-and-drop, but the interface is more complex because it does more. The template library is larger and more varied, the editor has more options (conversion widgets, payment forms, countdown timers, pop-up triggers), and the analytics dashboard adds another layer to learn. Most users need a few hours to feel comfortable, and getting the most out of A/B testing and Leadmeter requires ongoing learning.

## FAQ

### Can Carrd replace Leadpages?

For simple landing pages with a signup form, yes. For serious lead generation with A/B testing, conversion optimization, and analytics, no. Carrd has no split testing, no conversion analytics, and no AI optimization. If your landing page directly generates revenue through paid traffic, Leadpages will pay for itself through better conversion rates.

### Can I build a multi-page website with either tool?

Leadpages lets you build multiple landing pages and link them together, but it is not a traditional website builder. Carrd is strictly one-page sites. For a full multi-page website, neither is the right tool -- look at WordPress, Webflow, or Squarespace.

### Does Carrd support e-commerce?

Carrd supports payment forms through Stripe and PayPal on Pro Standard and above. You can sell single products or accept payments. But there is no shopping cart, inventory management, or product catalog. For actual e-commerce, you need a different tool.

### Is the Leadpages 14-day trial really free?

Yes, with full access to all features on whichever plan you choose. You do need to enter payment information, and you will be charged if you do not cancel before the trial ends.

### Can I use my own domain with Carrd?

Yes, on Pro Standard ($19/year) and above. Carrd provides free SSL certificates via Let's Encrypt for all custom domains.
