## The details

**Pricing:** SpyFu is roughly 70% cheaper at every tier ($39 vs $140, $119 vs $250, $249 vs $500). But Semrush bundles tools you'd pay for separately elsewhere. If you only need competitor research, that gap is hard to justify. If you need site audits, backlinks, and content tools too, Semrush can actually save money overall.

**PPC research:** SpyFu's home turf. It shows every Google Ad a competitor has ever run, their estimated budget, their best keywords, and how their strategy changed over time. Semrush covers PPC too, but with less historical depth. SpyFu treats PPC as the main event; Semrush treats it as one tool among many.

**SEO depth:** Semrush wins everywhere else. Site auditor crawling 100K+ pages, 43T+ backlink database, content optimization tools, rank tracking, keyword gap analysis. SpyFu doesn't offer site audits or backlink analysis at all.

**Ease of use:** SpyFu is simpler because it does less. Enter a domain, get results. Semrush has a real learning curve but Semrush Academy helps. Most users need a few weeks to feel comfortable with the full toolkit.

**The combo play:** Many marketers run both. SpyFu Basic ($39) + Semrush Pro ($140) = $179/mo, still cheaper than Semrush Guru alone ($250). Use SpyFu for PPC intel, Semrush for everything else.

## FAQ

**Is SpyFu accurate enough?** For competitive intelligence, yes. For traffic estimates, it's less precise than Semrush. All SEO tools work with estimates, not exact numbers.

**Can I use SpyFu instead of Semrush?** If you only need competitor keyword and PPC data, absolutely. If you need site audits, backlinks, or content tools, you'll still need Semrush or alternatives.

**Does Semrush have a free plan?** A limited free account with 10 searches/day. Enough for occasional checks, not enough for daily work. SpyFu shows partial data without signing up.
