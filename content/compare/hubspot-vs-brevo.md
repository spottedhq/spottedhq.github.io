**HubSpot** is the all-in-one platform for businesses that want CRM, marketing, sales, and service under one roof. It's polished, powerful, and expensive. The real features start at $800/mo.

**Brevo** charges by emails sent, not contacts stored. Unlimited contacts on every plan. A/B testing at $18/mo that costs $800/mo on HubSpot. For small businesses, the value is hard to argue with.

## The details

**Pricing:** The gap is massive. HubSpot Professional at $800/mo costs 44x Brevo Standard at $18/mo. Even at entry level, HubSpot Starter ($20/mo) is double Brevo Starter ($9/mo). HubSpot charges per marketing contact, so your bill grows as your list grows. Brevo charges per email sent, so you store unlimited contacts and only pay when you message them.

**Features:** HubSpot wins on CRM depth (deal pipelines, lead scoring, lifecycle stages), automation sophistication, reporting, and the all-in-one advantage. Brevo wins on price-to-feature ratio, multichannel messaging (email + SMS + WhatsApp in one place), and built-in transactional email. Brevo's Standard plan at $18/mo includes A/B testing. You need HubSpot Professional at $800/mo for the same thing.

**Ease of use:** Brevo is simpler. Most users send their first campaign within 30 minutes. HubSpot has a steeper learning curve because it does so much more. The CRM alone has properties, lifecycle stages, deal pipelines, and scoring to configure. Expect days, not hours, to get fully set up.

**The honest take:** If you're spending more on HubSpot than you're generating from it, switch to Brevo and reinvest the savings into your actual marketing. Start with HubSpot's free CRM to learn the ecosystem. Only jump to Professional when your revenue justifies it.

## FAQ

**Is the HubSpot free CRM really free forever?** Yes, with limits. 1K contacts, 2K emails/mo, HubSpot branding on everything. No time limits.

**Can Brevo replace HubSpot?** For email marketing and basic CRM, yes. For advanced CRM, sales pipelines, and enterprise reporting, no. If you only use HubSpot for email campaigns, switching to Brevo could save hundreds per month.

**Does Brevo include automation?** Yes, even on the free plan (limited). Standard at $18/mo includes visual workflows, A/B testing, and segmentation.
