**Fireflies.ai** is the better team meeting platform. Cross-meeting search, native Salesforce/HubSpot sync, video recording, and more platform support. Best for sales teams and organizations that treat meetings as data.

## Pick Otter.ai if...

You want live captions during meetings. You're an individual or small team focused on personal productivity. You attend lectures or in-person conversations you want transcribed. You don't need CRM integration.

## The details

**Pricing:** At comparable tiers, they're nearly the same. Otter Business at $20/mo and Fireflies Business at $19/mo. Otter gives more transcription minutes; Fireflies gives CRM sync and unlimited storage. Annual billing saves 40-50% on both.

**Live vs post-meeting:** This is the core difference. Otter's live captions are the best available. You see words appearing as people speak. Fireflies is stronger after the meeting: cross-meeting search, topic filtering, speaker filtering, and AI-generated topic segments.

**CRM integration:** Fireflies wins outright. Native Salesforce and HubSpot sync means meeting notes and action items flow into contact records automatically. For sales teams, this alone justifies picking Fireflies.

**Platform support:** Fireflies supports more meeting platforms natively, including Webex and GoTo Meeting. Otter focuses on the big three (Zoom, Meet, Teams). If your org uses Webex, the choice is made.

**Accuracy:** Both are strong for English. Otter's speaker identification is slightly better out of the box, especially for recurring speakers it has learned.

## FAQ

**Are the free plans useful?** Otter's (300 min/mo) is more practical for regular use. That's about 10 meetings at 30 minutes each. Both free plans work for testing but are limited for daily use.

**Do both join meetings automatically?** Yes. Connect your calendar and they join scheduled meetings as silent attendees.

**Can I use both on the same call?** Technically yes, but having two AI notetakers in one call is overkill and confuses participants. Pick one.
