## The details

**Pricing:** Kit's free plan changes everything. Up to 10,000 subscribers with unlimited emails, landing pages, and forms at $0. That's impossible to beat. ActiveCampaign Starter is cheaper than Kit Creator at small list sizes ($15 vs $29/mo at 1K contacts), but there's no free tier. At higher contact counts, ActiveCampaign's real cost often runs 30-50% above headline prices once you add SMS, transactional email, and reporting.

**Automation:** The biggest gap. ActiveCampaign is enterprise-grade: conditional splits, multi-channel sequences, goal-based triggers, predictive sending, machine learning optimization. Kit's automation is visual and intuitive but far simpler. If your business depends on sophisticated drip campaigns, ActiveCampaign is the only real choice.

**CRM:** ActiveCampaign includes a real CRM with deal pipelines, lead scoring, and win probability. Kit has no CRM. If you qualify leads and move deals through stages, ActiveCampaign serves double duty as marketing and sales platform.

**Email design:** Kit deliberately keeps emails text-focused. Simple, personal-feeling emails that avoid spam filters. ActiveCampaign offers a full drag-and-drop builder with templates and dynamic content. Different philosophies, both valid.

**Ease of use:** Kit is one of the easiest email platforms to learn. Most users send their first email within 30 minutes. ActiveCampaign requires real investment to learn. The automation builder alone takes days to master. The payoff is worth it for the right business, but the time cost is real.

## FAQ

**Is ConvertKit better than ActiveCampaign?** Neither is objectively better. Kit is better for creators who need simplicity. ActiveCampaign is better for businesses that need deep automation and CRM. Don't pay for complexity you won't use.

**Do I really need ActiveCampaign's automation?** Be honest. If you send a weekly newsletter, a welcome sequence, and occasional launches, Kit handles all of that. ActiveCampaign's advanced automation is worth it when you're doing lead scoring, behavioral triggers, and CRM-driven workflows.

**Can Kit sell digital products?** Yes. Built-in commerce for PDFs, courses, and paid newsletters. No Shopify or Gumroad required. ActiveCampaign has no equivalent.
