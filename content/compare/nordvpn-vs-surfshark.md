## What Is NordVPN?

NordVPN is the most recognized name in the consumer VPN space, and for good reason. Launched in 2012 by Nord Security (based in Panama for privacy-friendly jurisdiction), it has grown into a comprehensive cybersecurity suite serving over 15 million users worldwide.

At its core, NordVPN encrypts your internet traffic and routes it through secure servers, hiding your IP address and protecting your data from snooping -- whether that is your ISP, hackers on public Wi-Fi, or government surveillance. But NordVPN has evolved well beyond basic VPN functionality. Its Threat Protection feature blocks ads, trackers, and known malicious websites at the DNS level, even when the VPN is disconnected.

In 2026, NordVPN offers four plan tiers: Basic, Plus, Complete, and Prime. The Basic plan covers VPN-only functionality, while higher tiers bundle in a password manager (NordPass), data breach scanner, 1TB encrypted cloud storage (NordLocker), and even personal data removal services on the Prime plan. NordVPN supports up to 10 simultaneous device connections across all plans.

## What Is Surfshark?

Surfshark burst onto the VPN scene in 2018 and quickly earned a reputation as the best value VPN on the market. Headquartered in the Netherlands and operating under the jurisdiction of the British Virgin Islands, Surfshark has steadily expanded its server network and feature set while maintaining aggressive pricing that consistently undercuts competitors.

Surfshark's killer feature is **unlimited simultaneous connections**. While NordVPN caps you at 10 devices, Surfshark lets you protect every device in your household -- phones, laptops, tablets, smart TVs, routers -- all on a single subscription. For families or anyone with many devices, this alone can justify the choice.

In 2026, Surfshark offers three plan tiers: Starter, One, and One+. The Starter plan covers VPN and ad blocking. Surfshark One adds antivirus protection, webcam security, and real-time data breach alerts. Surfshark One+ includes everything in One plus an identity protection service (Incogni) that removes your personal data from data broker databases. All plans come with a 30-day money-back guarantee and a 7-day free trial on mobile.

### Speed and Performance

Independent speed tests in 2026 show NordVPN with a slight but consistent edge. NordVPN retains roughly 81% of your base download speed on average, while Surfshark holds about 77%. On nearby servers, both deliver speeds fast enough for 4K streaming and large downloads without noticeable slowdown.

Where the gap widens is on distant servers. Connecting to servers in Australia or Japan from North America, NordVPN retains over 60% of both download and upload speeds. Surfshark drops more noticeably on long-distance connections, though it remains perfectly usable for streaming and browsing.

NordVPN's speed advantage comes partly from its proprietary NordLynx protocol (based on WireGuard) and a larger server network -- 7,400+ servers in 118 countries versus Surfshark's 3,200+ in 100 countries. More servers means less congestion.

### Security and Privacy

Both VPNs use AES-256 encryption, support WireGuard and OpenVPN protocols, and have independently audited no-logs policies. Both offer a kill switch that cuts your internet if the VPN connection drops.

NordVPN edges ahead with a few extras: Double VPN (routes traffic through two servers), Onion over VPN (combines VPN with Tor network), and dedicated IP addresses (available as an add-on). NordVPN's Threat Protection also works independently of the VPN connection, blocking ads and malware at the system level.

Surfshark counters with its own strengths: MultiHop (similar to Double VPN), Camouflage Mode (disguises VPN traffic to bypass VPN blocking), and NoBorders Mode (works in restrictive countries). Surfshark's CleanWeb feature blocks ads and trackers, though it only works while the VPN is active.

Both are solid choices for privacy. NordVPN has been through more third-party audits, which gives it a slight trust advantage for the security-conscious.

### Streaming and Geo-Unblocking

Both VPNs reliably unblock Netflix, BBC iPlayer, Disney+, Hulu, and Amazon Prime Video in 2026. NordVPN is slightly more consistent with niche streaming services and sports platforms thanks to its larger server fleet, but Surfshark holds its own for the major services.

Neither VPN guarantees access to every streaming library at all times -- streaming services actively block VPNs, and access can change week to week.

### Ease of Use

Both apps are well-designed and beginner-friendly across Windows, macOS, iOS, Android, and Linux. NordVPN has a polished map-based interface that makes it easy to pick a server visually. Surfshark uses a cleaner, list-based design that some users find faster to navigate.

Setup takes under five minutes on both. Neither requires technical knowledge to get started. Both offer browser extensions for Chrome and Firefox.

## Who Should Use What?

**Pick NordVPN if:** you want the fastest speeds, the largest server network, and the most advanced security features. You are willing to pay a modest premium for consistently top-ranked performance. You connect 10 or fewer devices and value features like dedicated IPs or Onion over VPN.

**Pick Surfshark if:** you want the best value for money, especially on a 2-year plan. You have a household full of devices and need unlimited connections. You want solid VPN protection without paying a premium, and you do not need the absolute fastest speeds on distant servers.

## FAQ

### Is NordVPN better than Surfshark?

NordVPN is faster, has more servers, and offers more advanced security features. Surfshark is cheaper and allows unlimited devices. For most individual users, NordVPN is the better overall product. For families or budget-focused users, Surfshark offers more value per dollar.

### Can I use either VPN for streaming?

Yes. Both NordVPN and Surfshark reliably unblock major streaming services including Netflix US, BBC iPlayer, Disney+, and Hulu. NordVPN is slightly more consistent across niche platforms.

### Do these VPNs work in China?

Both have features designed to bypass VPN restrictions (NordVPN's obfuscated servers, Surfshark's NoBorders Mode), but neither guarantees reliable access in China. Performance varies and depends on the current state of the Great Firewall. If China access is critical, check both providers' latest status pages before subscribing.

### Is a 2-year VPN plan worth it?

If you are committed to using a VPN long-term, absolutely. The savings are substantial -- Surfshark drops from $15.45/mo to $1.99/mo, and NordVPN goes from $12.99/mo to $3.39/mo. Just be aware that these prices lock in for the initial term but may renew at a higher rate.
