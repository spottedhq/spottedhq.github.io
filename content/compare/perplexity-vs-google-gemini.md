## The details

**Pricing:** Nearly identical at the Pro level ($20 vs $19.99/mo). The difference is what you get. Perplexity gives you an elite research tool. Gemini gives you an AI assistant in every Google app. At the premium tier, Gemini Ultra (~$42/mo) is much cheaper than Perplexity Max ($200/mo).

**Source trust:** Perplexity cites every claim with clickable links. You can verify anything. Gemini occasionally links to sources but isn't consistent. For fact-finding where accuracy matters, Perplexity is the safer bet.

**Deep Research:** Both offer it. Perplexity's searches the web, reads hundreds of sources, and returns structured reports with citations in 2-4 minutes. Gemini's is better for analyzing your own documents and data. Different strengths.

**Versatility:** Gemini does more things. Image analysis, video understanding, code generation, Workspace integration. Perplexity does one thing better. If you need both research and daily productivity AI, using both at $40/mo total is a reasonable call.

## FAQ

**Which is more accurate?** For factual questions, Perplexity. Every answer is grounded in cited sources. Gemini's raw reasoning is comparable but verification is harder.

**Does Perplexity use Google's models?** No. It uses Claude, GPT-4, and its own models. Pro subscribers can choose which model powers their queries.

**Can I use Perplexity for academic research?** Yes, and it's one of the best tools for it. There's a free Education Pro plan for students and educators. Always verify citations against primary sources.
